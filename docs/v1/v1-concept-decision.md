# V1 Concept Decision

## Selected approach: Gentle Interview → Compass Workspace

V1 uses a hybrid of two concepts evaluated during Sprint 0.

### The Gentle Interview (V1 guided journey)

The entry experience at `/start`. One question or small cluster at a time, single-column, full-focus layout. The user never sees a form — they see a calm, intelligent system guiding them through what matters.

This is the right model for the first encounter because:
- The primary user is anxious, solo, often late at night, trying to make sense of something overwhelming
- One thing at a time eliminates cognitive overload
- The pace is the user's pace — skip and defer always available
- Emotional safety is maximised
- Safeguarding screening can be woven in precisely
- Mobile is trivially responsive
- Build complexity is low

### The Compass Workspace (persistent product)

The workspace at `/workspace` that the user enters after saving. A persistent sidebar route map showing the full journey (V1 chapters completed, V2–V5 chapters ahead) and a main area showing workspace cards generated from their inputs.

This is the right model for the returning user because:
- Transparency — they can see the whole journey and where they are
- Non-linear navigation — they can revisit and update any area
- Visual momentum — the route map fills in as they progress
- Workspace cards carry forward seamlessly into V2+
- No transition between "onboarding output" and "working product"
- Naturally extensible as verticals are added

### The transition

The Gentle Interview generates the workspace. When the user completes the guided journey and saves, they enter the Compass Workspace with their route map and cards already populated.

Returning users go straight to the workspace, not back through the interview.

### Three value deliverables from V1

1. **Process clarity** — understand what the separation/divorce process looks like for their specific situation. Guaranteed regardless of how much the user provides.
2. **A provisional plan** — shaped position on children, housing, and finances. Depth scales with what the user could provide (see v1-adaptive-output.md).
3. **Confidence map and next steps** — what they know vs. don't know, what matters most, and a prioritised action path forward.

### Concepts evaluated

| Concept | Description | Verdict |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| The Gentle Interview | One question at a time, single-column, maximum calm | Selected for the guided journey |
| The Compass | Chapter-based with persistent route map sidebar | Selected for the persistent workspace |
| The Clearing | Emerging canvas workspace, cards build as you answer | Elements merged into the workspace model |

The hybrid combines the emotional safety of the Gentle Interview with the structural clarity and extensibility of the Compass and Clearing concepts.
