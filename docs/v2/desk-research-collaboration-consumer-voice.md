## What people wish was different

1. **Understand the form without a solicitor** — the Form E is designed for lawyers, not people
2. **See what their ex disclosed** — until exchange day you work blind; even after, analysis requires expertise
3. **Stop paying for a process they can't see** — every email costs money; the client is last to know
4. **Speed up proposals** — the four-party chain adds weeks per round
5. **One place for everything** — documents scattered across emails, letters, solicitor files, ad-hoc Google Drives
6. **Know what's "normal" or "fair"** — the Law Commission confirmed nobody can predict outcomes
7. **Have the emotional dimension acknowledged** — "the process treats people as filing cabinets"
