# Workspace Design Spec — 07: Future Phase Pages

## URLs

- /workspace/disclose — Share & Disclose
- /workspace/negotiate — Work Through It
- /workspace/agree — Reach Agreement
- /workspace/finalise — Make It Official

## Purpose

These pages exist NOW (V2 build) even though their functionality comes in V3-V5. They are NOT empty pages. They are **educational, motivational, and commercially valuable.**

---

## What every future phase page shows

### 1. Phase header

Same warmth block as active phases, but with a "Coming soon" or "When you're ready" modifier.

```
SHARE & DISCLOSE
When your picture is complete, this is where you prepare to share it.
```

### 2. What this phase involves

Clear, bulleted explanation of what happens in this phase. Educational — answering the question "what comes after building my picture?"

```
What happens here
· Your financial picture gets structured into disclosure-ready format
· You and your partner exchange financial information
· Open questions are raised and tracked
· Everything links back to evidence
```

### 3. What you need first

Honest about prerequisites:

```
What you need first
· Key financial items confirmed with evidence
· Children's arrangements shaped (if relevant)
· Readiness level: "Ready for formal disclosure"
```

With a live status: "You're currently at 55% readiness. Keep building your picture."

### 4. Data emerging from earlier phases

If any data from V2 is relevant to this phase, show it:

For "Share & Disclose":
- "You have 5 confirmed items and 3 estimates that will need evidence before formal exchange."
- "Your CETV is still awaiting — this needs to arrive before disclosure."

For "Work Through It":
- "You've flagged fair split and pension protection as priorities. These will be the basis of your negotiation."

For "Reach Agreement":
- "No proposals yet. This phase activates when you begin negotiation."

### 5. How the service helps

The value proposition for the paid service at this stage:

```
How we help at this stage
· We structure your data into Form E-equivalent disclosure automatically
· Track what's been shared and what's outstanding
· Raise and manage open questions
· Keep everything linked to evidence
```

### 6. CTA

Primary: "Continue building your picture" (if not ready)
Or: "Enter this phase" (if readiness threshold is met)

Secondary: "View your plan" (links back to V1 plan)

---

## Visual design

These pages should feel complete and considered, not like placeholder stubs.

- Full warmth header block
- Content sections with clear structure
- Bullet points use teal checkmarks for "what happens" and warmth arrows for "how we help"
- The readiness status from V2 data is live and prominent
- Any emerging data from earlier phases is shown in teal-light info cards

---

## Differentiation between phases

Each future phase page has unique content tailored to that phase. Not a generic "coming soon" template repeated four times.

### Share & Disclose
Focus: disclosure, Form E, evidence linking, exchange process

### Work Through It
Focus: proposals, mediation sessions, tracking changes, negotiation memory

### Reach Agreement
Focus: resolving disputes, capturing final positions, getting to "yes"

### Make It Official
Focus: consent order, D81, Form A, court submission, solicitor handoff. Enhanced tier upsell.
