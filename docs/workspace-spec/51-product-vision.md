# Spec 51 — Product Vision

**Date:** 17 April 2026
**Status:** One-pager. The elevator pitch.
**Audience:** Anyone — investors, partners, team hires, yourself on a bad day.

---

## What Decouple is

**The complete settlement workspace for divorcing couples.** A single place to build a shared picture of your finances, children, home and future — together with your ex — and produce the legally binding documents that end your marriage.

---

## Who it's for

**Primary:** Separating couples in the UK where at least one partner wants to work collaboratively. Roughly 85% of the 180,000+ divorces a year fall in this space. Within that:

- **60%** — "already aligned" or "need a nudge" → self-serve the full journey
- **25%** — moderate conflict → self-serve with mediator facilitation
- **15%** — high conflict or high complexity → structured disclosure feeds into solicitor-led litigation

**Secondary:** Mediators (as a partner platform that supercharges their practice) and family law solicitors (as infrastructure their clients use before they arrive).

---

## Why it exists

The current system fails divorcing couples:

- **12-18 months** to reach a settlement
- **£14,561** average cost per person
- **The four-party chain** (client → solicitor → solicitor → client) where every email costs money
- **No shared source of truth** — each side works from their own Form E
- **No evidence verification** — self-declared data reviewed by a solicitor hearing only one side
- **No collaboration layer** — proposals travel as Word letters for weeks

Most of what makes divorce expensive is administrative, not legal. **80% of the process is document gathering, compiling, and communicating.** We automate that. The remaining 20% — genuine legal judgment — stays with humans, but only when needed.

---

## The value proposition

**Shared, not adversarial** — One document, built together.
**Evidenced, not asserted** — Bank-connected, credit-verified, document-linked.
**End-to-end, not hand-off** — From first question to sealed consent order.

For the typical couple, Decouple delivers what currently takes £14,561 and 18 months in **£800-1,100 and 2-3 months**.

---

## How it works (30 seconds)

1. You answer 6 quick questions about your situation
2. You connect your bank — the engine finds your salary, mortgage, pensions, debts
3. Your financial picture builds as a shareable document
4. You invite your ex; they do the same
5. The two halves merge into your household settlement picture
6. You negotiate per-item proposals with structured reasoning
7. Agreement auto-generates your consent order and D81
8. Submit to court directly, or get optional professional review

## Why now

- **Open Banking** is mature and consumer-trusted (2020+)
- **Express Financial Remedy Pilot** (April 2025) accelerates disclosure requirements — favours structured data products
- **Law Commission reform** (Spring 2026) expected to move toward more formulaic settlement — favours structured data products
- **AI-assisted legal tools** have crossed the trust threshold in 2025-26
- **Legal research** confirms consent orders can be self-submitted — no solicitor legally required for the standard case
- **Cost-of-living pressure** makes £15k legal bills unsustainable for most families
